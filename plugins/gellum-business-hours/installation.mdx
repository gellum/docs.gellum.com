---
title: "Installation"
description: "How tu install Gellum Business Hours"
---

1. Upload the `gellum-business-hours` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Navigate to **WooCommerce \> Business Hours** in your WordPress admin panel
4. Configure your store's operating hours for each day of the week
5. Save your settings

## Configuration

### Setting Up Business Hours

1. Go to **WooCommerce \> Business Hours** in your admin dashboard
2. For each day of the week, you can:
   - Set the status to **Open** or **Closed**
   - Define opening time (in 15-minute intervals)
   - Define closing time (in 15-minute intervals)
3. Click **Save changes** to apply your settings

### Overnight Hours

The plugin supports overnight business hours. If your store closes after midnight:

1. Set the opening time for the first day (e.g., Monday 22:00)
2. Set the closing time for the first day that is earlier than the opening time (e.g., 02:00)
3. The plugin will automatically recognize this as an overnight schedule
